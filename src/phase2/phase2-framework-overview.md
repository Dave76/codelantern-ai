## The A2D Delivery Methodology

CodeLantern engagements follow a disciplined **AI-Augmented Delivery (A2D)** methodology that ensures speed, safety, and repeatability.

### 1. AI-Accelerated, Human-Reviewed
AI agents and copilots accelerate code generation, testing, and documentation — but every deliverable undergoes human review for architectural integrity, security, and maintainability.  
We don’t “vibe code.” We deliver **reviewed, traceable, and production-ready** outcomes.

### 2. Secure by Design
Security and compliance are integrated from day one.  
Every artifact — from design diagrams to code commits — adheres to client security requirements, licensing guidelines, and privacy standards.  
AI outputs are scanned, reviewed, and verified before merging into mainline code.

### 3. Transparent and Traceable
All AI-generated content is version-controlled, timestamped, and linked to the project manifest.  
Clients can see exactly how AI was used — including prompts, iterations, and commit provenance — ensuring complete transparency across the delivery lifecycle.

### 4. Outcome-Focused
We don’t measure success by hours or story points, but by **delivered value**.  
Our goal is to help product owners reach meaningful outcomes faster — whether that’s launching an MVP, modernizing a legacy system, or operationalizing a new AI-driven capability.

Each CodeLantern engagement blends human expertise and AI capability within a structured team model.

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
