# Consulting Agreement Summary

This document outlines the key terms of the consulting arrangement between **CLC** and the **Consultant**.

## 1. Term of Engagement

- This engagement is effective from **January 1, 2026 to December 31, 2026**.
- Either party may terminate this agreement with **30 calendar days’ written notice**.

## 2. Responsibilities

- **Contributing to the development of the CodeLantern A2D methodology**, including the supporting toolkit, processes, and underlying technology.
- **Supporting the creation and refinement of brand awareness initiatives**, including sales collateral, presentations, demos, and other go-to-market materials.
- **Providing guidance and support to delivery consultants**, and when needed, **participating in early client implementations** to help ensure successful adoption of CodeLantern solutions.
- **Helping to identify, evaluate, and onboard delivery consultants** to support the growth of the CodeLantern team.
- **Participating in pre-sales activities**, including discovery sessions, solution shaping, and client presentations.

## 3. Compensation Plan

- CLC agrees to pay the Consultant (or the Consultant’s incorporated company) up to **$16,000 per month** for full-time services.
- The Consultant will submit an invoice at the end of each month for the amount owed, based on the **actual services performed during that month**, up to the monthly maximum.
- **CLC will reimburse reasonable business-related expenses**, provided they are **approved in advance**, and **receipts are included** with the corresponding invoice.

## 4. Bonus

- At the end of the engagement, the Consultant **may be eligible for a discretionary bonus**.
- Any bonus amount will be based on:
  - The Consultant’s **overall contribution, effort, and positive influence** on the company’s success.
  - The **financial performance of the company**.
- A bonus is **not guaranteed**.
- **At the company’s discretion, any bonus awarded may be applied toward the purchase of equity in the company** at the company’s current valuation.
- **The bonus amount and the related equity purchase opportunity will be determined by the company.**

## 5. Confidentiality

- The Consultant agrees to keep all non-public information received from CLC strictly confidential.
- Confidential information includes business plans, financial data, client information, intellectual property, internal methodologies, and proprietary tools.
- Confidential information may only be used for the purposes of performing this engagement.

## 6. Ownership of Work

- All work products created by the Consultant in connection with this engagement will be the exclusive property of CLC.
- If any work cannot legally be considered work made for hire, the Consultant assigns all rights, title, and interest to CLC.

## 7. Independent Contractor Status

- The Consultant is an **independent contractor**, not an employee.
- The Consultant is responsible for all taxes and statutory deductions.

## 8. Future Agreements

- This agreement may be superseded or replaced by an official **CLC Consulting Agreement** if one is drafted in 2026.
- **Monetary terms outlined in this document will not be altered** in any future agreement.
