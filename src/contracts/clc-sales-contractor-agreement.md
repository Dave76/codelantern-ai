# Sales Contractor Agreement

This document outlines the key terms of the sales-focused contracting arrangement between **CLC** and the **Contractor**.

## 1. Term of Engagement

- This engagement is effective from **January 1, 2026 to December 31, 2026**.
- Either party may terminate this agreement with **30 calendar days’ written notice**.

## 2. Responsibilities (Sales Role)

- **Supporting the creation and refinement of brand awareness initiatives**, including sales collateral, presentations, demos, and other go-to-market materials.
- **Contributing to the company’s overall sales strategy**, including defining and refining the ideal customer profile (ICP) and identifying priority market segments.
- **Building and maintaining a short list of potential beta clients and early adopters**, and nurturing these relationships through early sales cycles.
- **Establishing and managing key partner relationships**, including relationships with **Microsoft** and other strategic ecosystem partners.
- **Assisting with product definition and pricing models** for services and potential related subscriptions.
- **Helping to identify, evaluate, and onboard delivery consultants** to support the growth of the CodeLantern team.

## 3. Compensation Plan (Sales Role)

- CLC agrees to pay the Contractor (or the Contractor’s incorporated company) a **base compensation of $x,xxx per month**.
- The Contractor will submit an invoice at the end of each month for the base amount.
- **CLC will reimburse reasonable business-related expenses**, provided they are **approved in advance**, and **receipts are included** with the corresponding invoice.

### 3a. Commission (Year 1 Only)

- For the first year of this engagement, the Contractor will participate in a **shared commission model** at a rate of **8% of recognized revenue** from approved client contracts.
- The commission model is **shared across the sales team**, preventing internal competition for opportunities.
- The **8% commission rate** and shared model apply **to Year 1 only**.
- All client contracts must be **reviewed and approved by the company** to ensure adequate margin.
- Commissions will be paid according to the company’s standard revenue recognition schedule.

## 4. Bonus

- At the end of the engagement, the Contractor **may be eligible for a discretionary bonus**.
- Bonus determination will be based on:
  - The Contractor’s **overall contribution and influence**.
  - The **financial performance of the company**.
- Bonus is **not guaranteed**.
- **At the company’s discretion, any bonus awarded may be applied toward the purchase of company equity** at the then-current valuation.
- **The bonus amount and the related equity purchase opportunity will be determined by the company.**

## 5. Confidentiality

- The Contractor agrees to keep all non-public information received from CLC strictly confidential.
- Confidential information may only be used for the purpose of performing responsibilities under this agreement.

## 6. Ownership of Work

- All work products created in connection with this engagement are considered **work made for hire** and belong exclusively to CLC.
- If any work cannot legally be considered work made for hire, it is assigned to CLC.

## 7. Independent Contractor Status

- The Contractor is an **independent contractor**, not an employee.
- The Contractor is responsible for all applicable taxes and statutory deductions.

## 8. Future Agreements

- This agreement may be superseded or replaced by an official **CLC Sales Contracting Agreement** if one is drafted in 2026.
- **The monetary terms in this document will not be altered** in any future agreement.
